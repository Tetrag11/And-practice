#DREAM #DESIGN #DELIVER

Academy of Art & Design School (AND) is Sri Lanka’s first arts and design academy where you can learn creative skills, create, innovate and explore design aspects in the world.


Read more about AND >

Not only Fashion, Interior and Graphic design, we are happy to introduce new design aspects such as Academic drawing, Academic painting, Calligraphy, Sculpture design, Glass design and Ceramic design. It’s our vision to bring the hidden creativity and innovation to the world. Instituto Cordella Roma, Italy, along with other international partnerships, holding hands with AND to accomplish the goals.
